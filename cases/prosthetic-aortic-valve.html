BIOL 334 Unknown Identification Guide

  1. Perform a Gram Stain
    • Gram-positive cocci → Step 2
    • Gram-positive rods → Step 5
    • Gram-negative rods → Step 6
  2. Catalase Test
    • Catalase positive → Step 3
    • Catalase negative → Step 4
  3. Coagulase Test
    • Positive → Staphylococcus aureus
    • Negative → Staphylococcus epidermidis
  4. Blood Agar
    • Alpha hemolytic → Viridans streptococci
  5. Gram-positive rods
    • Endospore positive → Bacillus cereus
  6. MacConkey Agar
    • Pink colonies → Step 7
    • Colorless colonies → Step 8
  7. Lactose Fermenters
    • Indole positive → E. coli
    • Sorbitol negative → E. coli O157:H7
    • Citrate positive → Klebsiella aerogenes
  8. Non-Lactose Fermenters
    • Oxidase positive → Pseudomonas aeruginosa
    • Urease positive → Proteus mirabilis
